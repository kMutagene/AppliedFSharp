SeedList(string)
Signature: string

--seedTQ arg comma separated list of explicit seed base pair encoding(s) in the format startTbpsT&startQbpsQ, e.g. '3|||.|&7||.||', where startT/Q are the indices of the 5' seed ends in target/query sequence and 'bps' the dot-bar base pair encodings. This disables all other seed constraints and seed identification.
